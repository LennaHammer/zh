 

## 词汇

可以从典型论文中发现场景和替换的词汇。

给你一个**直接套用公式**
This paper **proposes** a novel **method** to **solve** the **problem** of… 
本文**提出**一种新**方法**来**解决**……的**问题**。

把加粗词随便换：
propose → present / introduce / develop
method → model / framework / algorithm
problem → challenge / issue / limitation
solve → address / handle / overcome

### 摘要 Abstract

核心句式为研究背景→现有问题→本文方法→实验结果→研究价值的线性逻辑，句式简洁凝练，多为复合句+并列句。
- 背景：`
	- The dominant [领域] models are based on [现有方法] that include [核心组件].`/`
	- [领域] pre-training has been shown to be effective for improving many [相关任务].`
	- 例：*The dominant sequence transduction models are based on complex recurrent or convolutional neural networks that include an encoder and a decoder.*
- 问题：`
	- Such restrictions are sub-optimal for [任务]`, and could be very harmful when applying [方法] to [场景].`/`
	- The fundamental constraint of [问题] remains.`
- 方法：`
	- We propose a new [模型/架构], [名称], based solely on [核心机制], dispensing with [舍弃的方法] entirely.`/`
	- We introduce a new [模型] called [名称], which stands for [全称].`
- 结果：`
	- Experiments on [任务] show these models to be superior in [指标] while being [优势].`/`
	- [模型] obtains new state-of-the-art results on [任务], pushing [指标] to [数值].`
- 价值总结：`
	- We show that [模型] generalizes well to other tasks by applying it successfully to [新任务].`/`
	- [模型] is the first [类型] model that achieves state-of-the-art performance on a large suite of [任务].`
