## 词汇



**整理说明**：本词汇表基于《Attention Is All You Need》（Transformer）与《BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding》两篇NLP顶会论文筛选，同组词汇**语境通用、可直接互换**，覆盖模型设计、实验分析、结果论证、逻辑衔接全场景，适配论文摘要、方法、实验、讨论等模块写作。


一、动词类（核心研究动作，高频互换）
- propose 提出 → introduce 引入 / present 提出 / develop 开发 【语境】阐述模型创新点 【例句】propose the Transformer /introduce BERT pre-training framework
- use 使用 → employ 采用 / utilize 利用 / adopt 采纳 / be based on 基于【语境】描述模型组件、训练策略 【例句】employ multi-head attention /adopt masked language model
- improve 改进 → enhance 增强 / boost 提升 / advance 推进 / optimize 优化 / upgrade 升级 【语境】表述性能增益 【例句】enhance model accuracy /boost translation BLEU score /upgrade model structure
- show 显示 → demonstrate 证明 / illustrate 阐述 / present / verify 验证 / reveal 揭示 / prove 证实 / find 发现 / indicate 表明 【语境】论证实验结论、模型有效性 【例句】demonstrate bidirectionality importance /illustrate attention mechanism
- achieve 取得 → obtain 获得, reach 达到, attain 实现, realize 实现, establish 确立 【语境】描述实验成果、指标突破 【例句】achieve state-of-the-art results /reach higher accuracy /establish new SOTA
- include 包含 → comprise 由… 组成, consist of 由… 构成, incorporate 纳入, integrate 整合, fuse 融合 【语境】拆解模型结构 【例句】the encoder consists of sub-layers /incorporate residual connections /fuse context features
- evaluate 评估 → assess 评定, measure 测量, conduct 实施 【语境】描述实验流程 【例句】evaluate model variants /conduct ablation studies
- increase 增加 → raise 提升 【语境】调参、扩容相关 【例句】increase the model dimension /raise the pre-training batch size
- reduce 减少 → decrease 降低, lower 下调, alleviate 减轻, mitigate 缓解 【语境】优化模型缺陷、成本 【例句】reduce computational complexity /alleviate training overhead /mitigate pre-training mismatch

- train 训练 → pre-train 预训练, fine-tune 微调, adjust 调整 【语境】模型训练全流程 【例句】pre-train on unlabeled corpus /fine-tune for downstream tasks
- perform 执行 → carry out 实施, implement 实现 【语境】实验操作、模型落地 【例句】perform comparative experiments /implement the masking strategy
- describe 描述 → elaborate 详细阐述 【语境】细化模型、实验细节 【例句】describe training regime /elaborate ablation settings

## 句型
这两篇经典NLP论文（《Attention Is All You Need》《BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding》）的句式遵循**计算机科学顶会学术论文的标准范式**，句式结构规整、逻辑衔接紧密，以客观陈述、逻辑论证、实验说明为主，同时兼具技术细节的精准表述，两类论文的句式特征高度统一且各有侧重，以下按**论文核心模块**总结通用句式+专属句式，附典型例句参考：

一、摘要（Abstract）模块
核心句式为**研究背景→现有问题→本文方法→实验结果→研究价值**的线性逻辑，句式简洁凝练，多为复合句+并列句。
1. **背景引入**：`The dominant [领域] models are based on [现有方法] that include [核心组件].`/`[领域] pre-training has been shown to be effective for improving many [相关任务].`
   例：*The dominant sequence transduction models are based on complex recurrent or convolutional neural networks that include an encoder and a decoder.*
2. **提出问题**：`Such restrictions are sub-optimal for [任务]`, and could be very harmful when applying [方法] to [场景].`/`The fundamental constraint of [问题] remains.`
3. **方法提出**：`We propose a new [模型/架构], [名称], based solely on [核心机制], dispensing with [舍弃的方法] entirely.`/`We introduce a new [模型] called [名称], which stands for [全称].`
4. **结果陈述**：`Experiments on [任务] show these models to be superior in [指标] while being [优势].`/`[模型] obtains new state-of-the-art results on [任务], pushing [指标] to [数值].`
5. **价值总结**：`We show that [模型] generalizes well to other tasks by applying it successfully to [新任务].`/`[模型] is the first [类型] model that achieves state-of-the-art performance on a large suite of [任务].`

二、引言（Introduction）模块
句式层次更丰富，以**递进式、对比式**为主，穿插被动句、转折句，用于梳理研究脉络、突出本文贡献，常出现学术研究的经典衔接句式。
1. **研究现状梳理**：`[方法] have been firmly established as state of the art approaches in [领域] such as [任务].`/`There are two existing strategies for applying [技术] to [下游任务]: [策略1] and [策略2].`
2. **现有方法缺陷**：`[现有方法] typically factor computation along [维度], which inherently [问题1] and becomes critical at [场景].`/`The major limitation is that [现有方法] are [缺陷], and this limits the choice of [架构] that can be used during [阶段].`
3. **本文研究动机**：`In this work we propose [模型] to alleviate the previously mentioned [问题] by using [核心方法].`/`To train a [目标模型], we simply [方法], and then [目标].`
4. **贡献点提炼**：`The contributions of our paper are as follows: • We demonstrate the importance of [点1]; • We show that [点2]; • We advance [点3].`

三、相关工作（Related Work）模块
以**分类式陈述**为主，多用被动句、并列句，按研究方向分点梳理，句式具有高度重复性，便于清晰划分研究脉络。
1. **领域分类引入**：`There is a long history of [研究方向], and we briefly review the most widely-used approaches in this section.`/`[方向1] and its predecessor generalize [传统研究] along a different dimension.`
2. **方法对比陈述**：`[方法A] uses [核心机制], while [方法B] uses [另一机制].`/`Unlike [方法A], [方法B] is designed to [核心目标] by [方法].`
3. **现有研究局限**：`Similar to [模型], their model is [特征] and not [不足].`/`At least partly due to this advantage, [模型] achieved previously state-of-the-art results on [任务].`

四、模型架构（Model Architecture）模块
**技术细节表述的核心模块**，句式以**定义式、描述式、公式关联式**为主，大量使用定语从句、介词短语界定模型组件，精准描述层级、结构、参数，同时结合符号/公式衔接自然。
1. **模型整体定义**：`[模型] follows this overall architecture using [核心组件] for both the [模块1] and [模块2], shown in the [位置] of Figure [编号].`/`[模型]’s architecture is a [类型] [基础架构] based on the original implementation described in [参考文献].`
2. **组件层级描述**：`The [模块] is composed of a stack of N=[数值] identical layers. Each layer has two sub-layers: the first is [组件1], and the second is [组件2].`/`For a given [对象], its [属性] is constructed by summing the corresponding [子属性1], [子属性2], and [子属性3].`
3. **参数与公式衔接**：`We compute the matrix of outputs as: [公式].`/`In this work we employ h=[数值] parallel [组件], or heads. For each of these we use d_k=[数值].`/`[公式] where [符号] is the [定义] and [符号] denotes [含义].`
4. **机制设计说明**：`We employ a [机制] around each of the two sub-layers, followed by [操作].`/`We modify the [组件] to prevent [问题], ensuring that [效果].`

五、训练与实验（Training/Experiments）模块
**实验设计与结果陈述的核心**，句式分为**实验设置（陈述式）、实验过程（步骤式）、结果分析（对比式/结论式）**，大量使用表格关联句、数值表述句，逻辑严谨且可复现。
（一）实验设置：客观陈述，参数清晰
1. **数据与硬件**：`We trained on the standard [数据集] consisting of about [数量] [数据单位].`/`We trained our models on [硬件] with [参数]. Each training step took about [时间].`
2. **优化器与正则**：`We used the [优化器] with [参数1], [参数2] and [参数3].`/`We employ three types of regularization during training: • [正则1]; • [正则2]; • [正则3].`
3. **超参数设置**：`For each task, we selected the best [超参数] (among [数值1], [数值2]) on the [验证集].`/`We fine-tune for [epoch] with a learning rate of [数值] and a batch size of [数值].`

（二）实验结果：对比为主，结合表格/指标
1. **表格关联**：`Results are presented in Table [编号].`/`Table [编号] shows [实验内容] as well as results from [对比模型].`/`As shown in Table [编号], [模型] outperforms [对比模型] by [数值] on [指标].`
2. **指标陈述**：`On the [任务] task, the [模型] outperforms the best previously reported models by more than [数值] [指标], establishing a new state-of-the-art [指标] of [数值].`/`[模型A] significantly outperforms [模型B] across all tasks, especially those with very little training data.`
3. **趋势分析**：`Larger models lead to a strict accuracy improvement across all [数据集], even for [小数据集] which only has [数量] labeled training examples.`/`The [方法] performs worse than the [对比方法] on all tasks, with large drops on [具体任务].`

六、消融实验（Ablation Studies）模块
**控制变量论证**的专属句式，以**假设式、对比式、结论式**为主，核心是“改变单一变量，分析结果变化”，句式具有强逻辑性。
1. **实验设计**：`We demonstrate the importance of [组件] by evaluating [变体模型] using exactly the same [实验条件] as [基准模型].`/`To ablate the [方法], we apply the [对比方法] by [操作] without [改变的变量].`
2. **结果分析**：`Removing [组件] hurts performance significantly on [任务].`/`The best performing method [操作] which is only [数值] [指标] behind [基准模型].`/`Fine-tuning is surprisingly robust to different [变量], however, as expected, using only [策略] was problematic when applying [方法] to [任务].`

七、结论（Conclusion）模块
**总结升华**，句式以**概括式、展望式**为主，回归研究核心，总结成果并提出未来方向，语气客观且具有前瞻性。
1. **研究成果总结**：`In this work, we presented [模型], the first [类型] model based entirely on [核心机制], replacing [传统方法] with [本文组件].`/`Recent empirical improvements due to [技术] have demonstrated that [结论]. Our major contribution is further generalizing these findings to [新方向].`
2. **未来研究展望**：`We are excited about the future of [领域] and plan to apply them to other tasks.`/`We plan to extend the [模型] to problems involving [新场景] and to investigate [新机制] to efficiently handle [新问题].`

八、通用学术句式（全文档高频出现）
1. **被动句**：学术论文核心句式，客观表述研究行为，如`This is also in contrast to [研究], which is used [方法].`/`The training loss is the sum of the mean [指标1] and the mean [指标2].`
2. **转折/对比句**：`While [事实1], [事实2].`/`Unlike [A], [B] [行为].`/`Despite its simplicity, we demonstrate that [结论].`
3. **因果句**：`This makes it more difficult to [结果] due to [原因].`/`At least partly due to this advantage, [模型] achieved [结果].`
4. **引用句**：`as described in Section [编号]`/`refer readers to [参考文献] as well as excellent guides such as [资料]`/`as reported in [论文/数据集]`
5. **符号/术语界定**：`In this work, we denote [符号] as [含义], [符号] as [含义], and [符号] as [含义].`/`Throughout this work, a “[术语]” can be [定义], rather than [易混概念].`

两类论文的句式细微差异
1. 《Attention Is All You Need》作为**Transformer开山之作**，句式更偏向**“架构创新式”**，大量使用**设计描述、机制解释**的句式，公式与文字衔接更紧密；
2. 《BERT》作为**Transformer下游经典应用**，句式更偏向**“预训练+微调范式式”**，新增**预训练任务设计、下游任务适配**的专属句式，消融实验的对比句式更丰富。

整体而言，两篇论文的句式是**NLP/机器学习顶会（NeurIPS/ACL等）** 的典型范本，遵循“客观、精准、逻辑递进”的核心原则，句式的重复性和模块化特征显著，便于读者快速抓取核心信息。
