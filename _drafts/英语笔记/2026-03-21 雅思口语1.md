**问题**
- **Part 1（4–5分钟）** 日常问答：约**3–4个话题**，每个话题**3–5题**，合计**8–12题**（必考题：工作/学习、家乡、住宿、兴趣等）。
- **Part 2（3–4分钟）** 个人陈述：**1道题**（话题卡，1分钟准备+2分钟陈述）。卡上有 4 个提示要点。人物、地点、物品、经历。
- **Part 3（4–5分钟）** 深度讨论：与Part 2话题相关，**5–8题**（深度讨论）。列举，认为，过去，比较，未来。
- **单场总计**：总时长 11–14分钟，**14–21题**（通常在**16–18题**）。




**评分标准**
1. 流利和连贯：
	- 流畅：不卡顿
	- 连接词：and, but, because, although, however 等
	- 结构：观点→原因→例子→总结
2. 词汇：
	- 不只是简单词
	- 替换
3. 语法：
	- 复杂句：从句、倒装、虚拟、完成时
	- 时态（过去时），单复数。
4. 发音：
	- 清晰
	- 单词发音、重音、语调、连读、弱读

### 话题和问法
### Part 1 日常问答
（4–5分钟）


简短、生活化、围绕个人经历与习惯。



Part 1 常见问法：
1. 基础信息 What’s your name?/Where are you from?
2. 喜好/习惯 Do you like…?/Do you often…?
3. 频率 How often do you…?
4. 偏好选择 Which do you prefer…?
5. 原因 Why do you…?
6. 过去/将来 Did you… when you were young? Will you… in the future?
7. 一般观点 What do you think of…?

 高频话题
- 开场身份核对（必问） - What’s your full name? - Where are you from? - Do you work or study?
- **学习/工作** - What subject are you studying?/What do you do for work? - Do you enjoy your studies/job? Why?
- **家乡/住所** - What’s your hometown like? - Do you live in a house or an apartment? - What do you like most about where you live?
- **兴趣/休闲** - What do you do in your free time? - Do you prefer reading or watching movies? - How often do you do sports?
- **生活习惯** - Do you usually eat breakfast? - What’s your favorite type of weather? - Have you ever cooked for your family?

 常见问法
- Do you like…?/Are you into…?
- What do you think of…?
- How often…?/When…?
- Which do you prefer… or…?
- Have you ever…?

### Part 2 个人陈述
（1分钟准备 + 2分钟独白）
**核心问法**：**话题卡（Cue Card）+ 4个提示要点**，要求完整、连贯地描述人物、地点、事件、物品、经历等。
 话题卡结构（固定格式）
- Describe a [person/place/thing/event/experience]…
- You should say:
	- What it is/Who it is
	- Where/When/How you knew it
	- What you did/What it is like
	- And explain why it is important/memorable/special to you

 高频话题
- **人物** - Describe a person who has influenced you. - Describe a famous person you’d like to meet.
- **地点** - Describe a place you visited that impressed you. - Describe a quiet place you like.
- **物品** - Describe something you own that is very important. - Describe a gift you received.
- **经历/事件** - Describe a memorable trip you took. - Describe a time you helped someone.

### Part 3 深度讨论
（4–5分钟）

基于 Part 2 话题延伸，**抽象、思辨、社会层面**，以 **Why/How/What/Do you think…?** 为主，考察逻辑与批判性思维。
 典型问法
- **对比/优劣** - What are the advantages and disadvantages of…? - Do you think A is better than B? Why?
- **原因/影响** - Why do people…? - How does… affect our daily lives?
- **趋势/预测** - How do you think… will change in the future? - What trends do you see in…?
- **观点/评价** - Do you think we spend too much time on…? - What’s your opinion on…?
- **解决方案** - How can we solve the problem of…? - What should governments do to…?

- Part 2：Describe a memorable trip
- Part 3：
	- Why do people like traveling?
	- How has tourism changed your country?
	- Do you think travel makes people more open-minded? Why?

## 作答结构

整体思路
- P1 
	- 每题回答2–3句话，5–8秒。
	- 直接回答 + 简单补充（原因/影响/举例）。
	- 例 Do you like reading? Yes, I like reading novels. It helps me relax after a long day.
	- 例如 Do you like reading? Yes, I like reading. I usually read books before sleep. It helps me relax.
	- 常用理由：放松（relax myself）、减压（release my pressure）、保持健康（keep fit）、开阔视野（broaden my horizon）、保护环境（protect the environment），节约能源（conserve the natural resources）等等。
	- 内容先概括再具体的顺序展开细节。从概括词（名词、形容词）开始。
- P2 
	- 准备 1分钟 回答 1–2分钟，尽量说满2分钟。连贯说12–18句。
	- 涉及是什么、描述怎么样、有什么经历、原因为什么喜欢。
	- 结构   1. 开头点题（1句）I’d like to talk about ...  2. 按提示卡4点说（每点2–3句）  3. 结尾总结（1句）
- P3  
	- 每问回答4–6句话，20–30秒。 
	- 回答 观点 + 解释/原因 + 举例/对比



结构


 Part 1（简单日常题，一问一答）：答案 + 原因 + 例子 + 感受
- **万能4步结构（30秒左右）** 1. 直接回答（Yes/No/A little/Definitely） 2. 原因（because…） 3. 例子/细节（for example） 4. 感受/补充（personally/to be honest）
- 例句：Do you like reading? - Definitely.（答） - Because it helps me relax and broaden my horizons.（原因） - I usually read novels before going to bed.（例子） - It’s actually one of my favourite hobbies.（补充）
- 避坑：不要只说 Yes/No，太短会低分。



 Part 2（独白，1–2分钟，最重要）：是什么 + 为什么 + 经过 + 感受（加细节）
- **经典：PREP + 时间线/细节** 标准结构（照着说就够2分钟） 1. Point：先说是什么（人物/地点/物品/事件） 2. Reason：为什么喜欢/印象深 3. Example：具体发生了什么（过程、细节） 4. Feeling：你的感受、收获、影响
- 再加 **4个加分细节**（必加） - 时间：when - 地点：where - 人物：who - 对比：compared with…
- 简易开场模板 I’d like to talk about…/Well, the one that comes to mind is…



 Part 3（深度讨论，抽象问题，最难拿分）：观点 + 两点理由 + 例子/对比
- 适合 **社会、观点、对比、趋势类问题**
- **万能3段结构** 1. 观点开头 I think/From my perspective… 2. 分论点 + 解释（两点最好） Firstly,… because… Secondly,… which means… 3. 举例/对比/未来趋势 For instance…/In the past… but now…/In the future…
 - 例句：Should children learn art at school? - Personally, I believe it is quite necessary.（观点） - Firstly, art can boost children’s creativity, which benefits their study.（分论点1） - Also, it helps them relieve pressure from heavy coursework.（分论点2） - For example, many primary schools offer painting classes now.（例子）

一、万能结构（永远 3 步） 1. 直接回答（一句话给观点） 2. 解释原因/举例（1–2 句） 3. 拓展：对比/结果/未来（1 句）

 

二、每类题的万能句式

1. 对比题 What’s the difference between A and B?
	- Well, the main difference is that…
	- A is more…, but B is more…
	- I think this is because…
2. 同意/不同意 Do you agree that…?
	- Yes, I totally agree, because…
	- No, I don’t think so, the main reason is…
	- Also, …
3. 好处/坏处 What are the advantages? What are the disadvantages?
	- The biggest advantage is that…
	- It also helps people to…
	- As a result, …
	- The main problem is that…
	- It may cause…
4. 为什么 Why do people…?
	- I think the main reason is that…
	- Another reason is that…
	- So that’s why…
5. 未来/变化 How will it change in the future?
	- I think it will become more…
	- Because technology is developing…
	- Maybe people will…
6. 解决方案 How can we solve this problem?
	- I think the government/people should…
	- Also, it’s important to…
	- If we do this, …
