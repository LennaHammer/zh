特殊句型
- 动词
	- give: She gave her ticket to the woman at the check-in desk.
	- go: She went into her room and shut the door behind her.
	- put: Did you put sugar in my coffee?
	- look: She looked at me and smiled.
	- try:  You haven’t even tried to find it.
- 副词
	- very: I'm very very grateful.
	- often: We often go there.
	- never:
	- ever:
	- all
- 形容词
	- good: a good book. The results were pretty good.
	- interested: I'm very interested in history.
	- interesting: The article raises several interesting questions.
	- red: a red dress
	- glad: I’m glad (that) you’re feeling better.
- 介词
	- of: a cup of coffee
	- at: look at, at home, 
	- after doing: 
- it句型： 
	- it's + A + to do: It's impossible to get there in time.
	- It's no use shouting.
	- She finds it boring at home.
- there be：
	- There's a restaurant around the corner.
	- There are two people waiting outside.
- 从句 
	- that: It’s the best novel *that* I’ve ever read.
	- when: I loved history when I was at school.
	- if: If you see him, give him this note.
- 比较句
	- as...as: You're as tall as your father.
	- than: I'm older than her.
- 数量 i'm ten years old. 
- 否定句（not）：
	- i don't know. i have no idea. i don't think so.
	- I didn't think people were allowed to park here.
- 被动句 by被：He was knocked down by a bus.
- 非谓语动词：
	- after:  介词加doing做状语：
	- by: Switch it on by pressing this button.
	- to:  I set out to buy food.
- 虚拟语气
	- if: If he improved his IT skills, he'd (= he would) easily get a job.
	- if: They would have been here by now if they'd caught the early train.
	- wish：i wish i could.

	- tell: Did she tell you her name?
- 我认为 I think: I think this is their house, but I'm not sure.
