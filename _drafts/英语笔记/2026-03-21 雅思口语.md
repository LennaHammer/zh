## 句型

Part 1 万能句型（2–3句就行）
- 开头： - Well, I think . - Actually, . - To be honest, …
- 表达喜好： - I really like . because . - I’m quite into . - I prefer . rather than …
- 说频率： - I usually . - I often . - Sometimes I . - I rarely …
- 直接套示例： A: Do you like reading? B: To be honest, I really like reading because it helps me relax. I usually read novels before I go to bed.

Part 2 万能结构（撑满2分钟）
- 开头： - I’d like to talk about . - The [thing/person] I want to describe is …
- 点1：介绍： - It is . - He/She is . - I’ve known him/her for …
- 点2：细节： - What I usually do is . - I remember that . - The reason why . is that …
- 点3：感受： - I feel really . - It was such a . experience - I really enjoy it because …
- 结尾： - That’s why I [like/love] it so much.

Part 3 万能句型（4–6句）
- 表达观点： - I think . - In my opinion, . - From my perspective, …
- 对比： - Compared with …, . - In the past, . but now …
- 说原因： - The main reason is that . - This is because …
- 说影响： - It has a big influence on . - It can help people to …
- 总结： - Overall, . - In general, …

## 问法

雅思口语分 **Part 1（日常问答）、Part 2（个人陈述）、Part 3（深度讨论）**，问法、话题、难度逐级提升。

---

 Part 1 日常问答（4–5分钟）


简短、生活化、围绕个人经历与习惯。


 高频话题与典型问法
- 开场身份核对（必问）
	- What’s your full name?
	- Where are you from?
	- Do you work or study?
- **家乡/住所**
	- What’s your hometown like?
	- Do you live in a house or an apartment?
	- What do you like most about where you live?
- **学习/工作**
	- What subject are you studying? / What do you do for work?
	- Do you enjoy your studies/job? Why?
- **兴趣/休闲**
	- What do you do in your free time?
	- Do you prefer reading or watching movies?
	- How often do you do sports?
- **生活习惯**
	- Do you usually eat breakfast?
	- What’s your favorite type of weather?
	- Have you ever cooked for your family?

 常见句式
- Do you like…? / Are you into…?
- What do you think of…?
- How often…? / When…?
- Which do you prefer… or…?
- Have you ever…?

---

 Part 2 个人陈述（1分钟准备 + 2分钟独白）
**核心问法**：**话题卡（Cue Card）+ 4个提示要点**，要求完整、连贯地描述人物、地点、事件、物品、经历等。
 话题卡结构（固定格式）
- Describe a [person/place/thing/event/experience]…
- You should say:
	- What it is / Who it is
	- Where / When / How you knew it
	- What you did / What it is like
	- And explain why it is important / memorable / special to you

 高频话题与示例
- **人物**
	- Describe a person who has influenced you.
	- Describe a famous person you’d like to meet.
- **地点**
	- Describe a place you visited that impressed you.
	- Describe a quiet place you like.
- **物品**
	- Describe something you own that is very important.
	- Describe a gift you received.
- **经历/事件**
	- Describe a memorable trip you took.
	- Describe a time you helped someone.

---

 Part 3 深度讨论（4–5分钟）
**核心问法**：基于 Part 2 话题延伸，**抽象、思辨、社会层面**，以 **Why/How/What/Do you think…?** 为主，考察逻辑与批判性思维。
 典型问法类型
- **对比/优劣**
	  - What are the advantages and disadvantages of…?
	  - Do you think A is better than B? Why?
- **原因/影响**
	  - Why do people…?
	  - How does… affect our daily lives?
- **趋势/预测**
	  - How do you think… will change in the future?
	  - What trends do you see in…?
- **观点/评价**
	  - Do you think we spend too much time on…?
	  - What’s your opinion on…?
- **解决方案**
	  - How can we solve the problem of…?
	  - What should governments do to…?

 2. 示例（对应 Part 2 话题）
- Part 2：Describe a memorable trip
- Part 3：
	- Why do people like traveling?
	- How has tourism changed your country?
	- Do you think travel makes people more open-minded? Why?

## 答题结构


- P1：答案 + 原因 + 例子 + 感受
- P2：是什么 + 为什么 + 经过 + 感受（加细节）
- P3：观点 + 两点理由 + 例子/对比


 Part 1（简单日常题，一问一答）
**万能4步结构（30秒左右）**
1. 直接回答（Yes / No / A little / Definitely）
2. 原因（because…）
3. 例子 / 细节（for example）
4. 感受 / 补充（personally / to be honest）

例句：Do you like reading?
- Definitely.（答）
- Because it helps me relax and broaden my horizons.（原因）
- I usually read novels before going to bed.（例子）
- It’s actually one of my favourite hobbies.（补充）

避坑：不要只说 Yes / No，太短会低分。

---

 Part 2（独白，1–2分钟，最重要）
**经典：PREP + 时间线 / 细节**
标准结构（照着说就够2分钟）
1. Point：先说是什么（人物/地点/物品/事件）
2. Reason：为什么喜欢/印象深
3. Example：具体发生了什么（过程、细节）
4. Feeling：你的感受、收获、影响

再加 **4个加分细节**（必加）
- 时间：when
- 地点：where
- 人物：who
- 对比：compared with…

简易开场模板 I’d like to talk about… / Well, the one that comes to mind is…

---

 Part 3（深度讨论，抽象问题，最难拿分）
适合 **社会、观点、对比、趋势类问题**
**万能3段结构**
1. 观点开头 I think / From my perspective…
2. 分论点 + 解释（两点最好） Firstly,… because… Secondly,… which means…
3. 举例 / 对比 / 未来趋势 For instance… / In the past… but now… / In the future…

例句：Should children learn art at school?
- Personally, I believe it is quite necessary.（观点）
- Firstly, art can boost children’s creativity, which benefits their study.（分论点1）
- Also, it helps them relieve pressure from heavy coursework.（分论点2）
- For example, many primary schools offer painting classes now.（例子）
