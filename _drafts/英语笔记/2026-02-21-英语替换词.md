## 简单句替换

短句可以合并成长句（比如最简单的方式是用 and 连接），可以补充细节。

短句合并成长句
1. 用 and / but / so 合并（并列句）
	- I like reading. I like music. → I like reading and music.
	- He is tired. He keeps working. → He is tired but he keeps working.
	- It was raining. We stayed at home. → It was raining so we stayed at home.
2. 用 because / when / if 合并（状语从句）
	- She didn’t come. She was ill. → She didn’t come because she was ill.
	- I got home. I called you. → When I got home, I called you.
	- You study hard. You will pass. → If you study hard, you will pass.
3. 用定语从句合并（who / which / that）
	- I have a friend. He speaks English well. → I have a friend who speaks English well.
	- This is a book. It is very interesting. → This is a book which is very interesting.
4. 用非谓语动词（to do 为了 / doing 主动 / done 被动）合并（高级写法）
	- I am happy. I meet you. → I am happy to meet you.
	- She sat there. She read a book. → She sat there reading a book.
	- The window was broken. It was fixed. → The broken window was fixed.
5. 用介词短语合并
	- He left. He didn’t say goodbye. → He left without saying goodbye.
	- I bought a pen. It cost 10 yuan. → I bought a pen for 10 yuan.


2个简单句 → 1个长句

用 **and / but / so** 变并列句
- I like English. It is useful.→ I like English, **and** it is useful.
- He worked hard. He failed.→ He worked hard, **but** he failed.
- It rained. We stayed at home.→ It rained, **so** we stayed at home.

从句
1. 用 **because / when / if / though** 变状语从句
	- He was late. He missed the bus.→ He was late **because** he missed the bus.
	- I got home. I called you.→ I called you **when** I got home.
	- You study hard. You will pass.→ **If** you study hard, you will pass.
2. 用 **which / who / that** 变定语从句 
	- I have a friend. He speaks English well.→ I have a friend **who speaks English well**.
	- This is a book. It is interesting.→ This is a book **that is interesting**.
3. 简单句 ↔ 名词性从句（主语/宾语/同位语从句）
	- 宾语从句 I know. He is honest.→ I know **that he is honest**.
	- 主语从句 - English is important. It is true.→ **That English is important** is true.→ 更常用：**It is true that English is important.**

非谓语动词
1. 主动 doing
	- He stood there. He waited for me.→ He stood there, **waiting for me**.
2. 被动 done
	- The bridge was built last year. It is strong.→ The bridge **built last year** is strong.
3. 目的 to do
	- I got up early. I wanted to catch the bus.→ I got up early **to catch the bus**.


介词
介词+doing，作状语



位置
- 句首
- 句中，插入
- 句尾


口语说长句的**技巧**
4. **先说主干，再说细节** 不要一上来就堆修饰： ❌ Because when I was… I… ✅ I decided to study abroad **because I want to see the world**.
5. **停顿在连接词/从句前** 这样别人听得懂，你也不会乱： I think / that it’s important / to keep trying / even if you fail.
6. **少用and，多用连接词** ❌ I went shopping and I bought a shirt and it was cheap. ✅ I bought a shirt **which was cheap** when I went shopping.
7. **不要一直用 and / so**   换成其他连接词用 however, therefore, as a result, besides 。或者换成从句，用引导词。
8. **长短句搭配**   短句有力，长句丰富。
9. **先保证主句完整**   一个句子**必须有且只有一个主句谓语**，其他都用从句/短语包起来。



## 辨析

 - affect / effect： - affect v. 影响 The weather affected our plan. - effect n. 影响，效果 The effect was obvious.
- rise / raise - rise vi. 升起，上升（不及物，不加宾语） The sun rises. - raise vt. 举起，提高（及物，要加宾语） Raise your hand.
- advise / advice： - advise v. 建议 I advise you to study hard. - advice n. 建议（不可数） He gave me good advice.
- alone / lonely： - alone adj./adv. 独自（客观状态） I am alone but happy. - lonely adj. 孤独的（主观情感） I feel lonely.
- win / beat： - win 赢 + 比赛/奖品/战争 win the game - beat 打败 + 人/队伍 beat him
- take / spend / cost / pay： - It takes sb. time to do - sb. spends time/money on sth. - sth. costs sb. money - sb. pays money for sth.
