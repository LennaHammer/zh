### 介词短语

常见介词
- in
    - 在（时间）里: in the morning，在某月 _in May._，在某年 in 2000，in the 18th century，"in the late 19th century."
    - 在（地点）里面: in the room，in the schoolroom
    - 方式: "in this way"，_in English. in a loud voice. in the same way._ "in a shrill，passionate voice." "in a soothing tone"
    - 其他: "in her lessons" "in the distance，"，"in this way"，"in that case**在那种情况下**"，in general，"in a great hurry，"
- at 在某时刻: "at the time"，at noon，at once，"at some point"，at first，at last 在某地点: at the bus stop，at the airport
- on: 在某天 on Monday，on Sunday，on April 20，1990 在上面 on the desk，on some systems，on page two
- for+一段时间: for years 好多年，for the first time第一次，for three days，**for a long time .** 为了 因为for several reasons.
- with：with fright
- By the way
- 其他: after all 毕竟，the same as，as usual 照常
- 复合介词: instead of，in terms of，in addition to，according to根据，"away from"，in spite of

无介词的短语
- 副词："once or twice"，"sooner or later." … ago，two days later
- 名词："this time这次，""all the same，"，_all morning_**整个早上**_._ several times. "this afternoon." last week，，I've been there several times. every night


介词按功能分类。位置通常在句尾。
- **时间介词**：at，in，on，for，by不迟于，since 2010，"after a while，" "during this semester" I was expecting him here at five forty-five. 频率 two hours a week，时长 for many years，for one semester.in 1989，twenty-six years after the crash，By this time
- **地点介词**：，位置在句尾作状语，名词后：in，on，under，between，under the bed，in front of，He lives in Florida.
    - 位置关系：in the room 在…内部，on the table 在…表面，at the station 在…点，under the chair 在…正下方，below zero 在…下方，over the roof 在…正上方/覆盖，above the clouds 在…上方，beside her mother 在…旁边，next to the supermarket 紧挨着，between the school and hospital 在…两者之间，among the crowd 在…三者及以上之间，in front of the house 在…前面/外部，in the front of the bus 在…前部/内部，behind the truck 在…后面，against the wall 靠着/紧贴着，near the park 在…附近，far from the office 离…很远，inside the building 在…内部，outside the door 在…外部
    - 移动: across the street 穿过/表面，through the window 穿过/内部，along the river 沿着，around the lake 在…周围，beyond the hill 在…那边/超出，opposite the library 在…对面
- **方式介词**：by，with，in，by air，by boat，in this way
- 其他。原因/目的介词: for，because of，due to
- 名词后介词，地点，in，about关于，from，of
