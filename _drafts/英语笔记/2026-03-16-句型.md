it句：
It 作形式主语 **It takes** me 10 minutes to walk there.**It is** important that you study hard. **It is** easy to learn English. it would not be possible for us to ...
It 作形式宾语 I find **it** difficult to remember words.
It 指代时间、天气、距离：**It is** 7 o’clock. **It is** raining.
It 强调句 **It is** Tom **who** helped me.

  

there be句：**There is** a book on the desk. **There must be** a mistake.

  


**引语：直接**He said, “I am tired.”**间接** He said he was tired.

非谓语动词
不定式目的。I went out **to buy** some water.
现在分词表伴随。He sat there **reading** a book.
动词 + to do/doing：I want **to go**. I like **swimming**. 感受 Im gald to ....
过去分词：a book **called** Harry Potter
动名词：动词后 stop doing，介词后 be tired of doing，介词后 after doing 主语 Eating vegetables is good for health.
不定式和动名词可以有主语： for sb to do sth, one's doing sth.

  

  

时态
i‘ve been doing 一直在做：He **has been working** all day.
i have done 做完：She **has seen** the movie.
i did 过去发生 ：I **went** to school yesterday.
im going to do 将来，打算：I **am going to** visit my grandma.
im doing 正在做：I **am reading** a book.

  

从句
状语从句 when：I was happy **when I saw you**
定语从句 people who：The boy **who is reading** is my friend. The book **(that) I bought** is good.
名词从句 i think ：I think **you are right**. I'm glad **(that) you are here**. **What I need** is a pen.

  

数字：年龄 I am **16 years old**. 倍数 This room is **three times larger than** that one. 大约 It is **about 10 kilometers**. 增减 The number **increased by 20%**. 分数 **half an hour**, **one third** 运算 One **plus** two **is** three.
