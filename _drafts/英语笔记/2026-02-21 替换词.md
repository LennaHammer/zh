## 雅思口语替换词
- **喜欢/不喜欢**
    - like → enjoy, be fond of, be into
    - love → adore, be crazy about, have a passion for
    - dislike → can’t stand, be not fond of, hate
- **好/坏**
    - good → fantastic, awesome, terrific, decent
    - very good → pretty good, extremely good, remarkably good
    - bad → terrible, awful, lousy
    - very bad → absolutely terrible, pretty bad
- **重要/必要**
    - important → vital, crucial, essential, significant
    - necessary → indispensable, required
    - not important → trivial, insignificant
- **很多/很少**
    - many → a lot of, numerous, a great number of
    - much → a great deal of, a large amount of
    - few → hardly any, a handful of
    - little → scarce, barely any
- **认为/觉得**
    - I think → I believe, I reckon, I suppose, I feel
    - in my opinion → from my perspective, as far as I’m concerned
    - I think so → I guess so, I suppose so
- **经常/偶尔**
    - often → frequently, regularly, quite often
    - sometimes → occasionally, from time to time
    - always → invariably, all the time
    - never → hardly ever, rarely
- **过渡连接**
    - and → besides, additionally, what’s more
    - but → however, nevertheless, on the other hand
    - because → since, as, the reason is that
