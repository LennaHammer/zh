## 短语动词（按副词/介词分类）


按照 副词/介词 > 副词/介词的意思 细分。



### 🔄 about（关于/周围）
*about 表示"关于、涉及某话题"，常与言说、思考类动词搭配。*

- [ ] **talk about**  — 谈论，讨论 — *talk about your-  -big lizards...*
- [ ] **think about**  — 考虑；思考 — *I am just going to get up, go to work and not think about him all day.*
- [ ] **feel about**  — 对……感觉（如何） — *Look, I don't even know how I feel about him yet.*

### 👥 with（和……一起）
*with 表示"伴随、一起、带有"，强调共同参与或伴随状态。*

- [ ] **live with** — 与……一起生活；忍受 — *Ross, is he gonna live with you, like, in your apartment?*

### 🔼 up

*up 表示"向上、完成、彻底"，是最常见的短语动词小品词，强调动作的完结或增强。*

**向上/移动 (Going up)**
- [ ] **get up**  — 起床；站起来（=rise from bed）— *I am just going to get up, go to work and not think about him all day.*
- [ ] **stand up**  — 站起来（=rise to feet）
- [ ] **go up**  — 上升；上涨；走上前（go up to） — *You've just got to go up to her and say, 'Rachel, I think that...'*
- [ ] **run up**  — 跑上去；积欠（账单） — *You've gotta just keep thinking about the day that some kid is gonna run up to his friends and go 'I got the part!*
- [ ] **pick up (something/someone)**  — 捡起；接人；购买；学会（=collect; learn）— *I should shave again, pick up some wine, what?*
- [ ] **wake (someone) up**  — 醒来（vi）；叫醒（vt）（=stop sleeping）— *Yeah, right! See, he gave up something, but then he got those magic beans. And then he woke up, and there was this, this big plant outside his window, full of possibilities and stuff.. And he lived in a village, and you live in the Village..*
- [ ] **grow up**  — 长大（=become an adult）— *So, where did you guys grow up?*

**完成/结束 (Completing/Ending)**
- [ ] **break up (with someone)**  — 分手；结束关系（=end a relationship）— *I've decided to break up with Alan.*
- [ ] **shut up**  — 闭嘴（=be quiet）— *Shut up, Joey!*
- [ ] **end up (doing)**  — 最终（处于某种状态或结果）（=finally; wind up）— *You're welcome. I remember when I first came to this city. I was fourteen. My mom had just killed herself and my step-dad was back in prison, and I got here, and I didn't know anybody. And I ended up living with this albino guy who was, like, cleaning windshields outside port authority, and then he killed himself, and then I found aromatherapy. So believe me, I know exactly how you feel.*
- [ ] **give up (something)**  — 放弃（=quit; surrender）— *Jack gave up a cow, I gave up an orthodontist.*
- [ ] **clean up (something)**  — 清理；打扫（=tidy up）— *All right, you guys, I kinda gotta clean up now.*
- [ ] **mess up (something/someone)**  — 搞砸；弄乱；使困扰（=ruin; screw up）— *What's messing you up? The wine?*
- [ ] **catch up (with someone)**  — 赶上；叙旧（=get up to date）— *How about I'll, uh, catch up with you in the Ice Age.*

**出现/增强/打开**
- [ ] **come up**  — 出现；走过来；被提及（=arise; approach）— *Oh, you wouldn't know a great butt if it came up and bit ya.*
- [ ] **show up**  — 出现；露面（=appear; turn up）— *'That thing'? This is how you greet guests at a party? Let me ask you something, if I showed up here with my new girlfriend, she wouldn't be welcome in your home?*
- [ ] **speak up**  — 大声点说（=talk louder）— *Could you speak up please?*
- [ ] **hold up (someone/something)**  — 举起；耽搁；抢劫（=delay; rob）— *No, he said we were holding up the people behind us.*
- [ ] **open up (to someone)**  — 打开（something）；敞开心扉（=open; share feelings）— *I'm going through my mail, and I open up their monthly, you know, STATEMENT-*
- [ ] **well up**  — 涌出；涌上（情感）（=fill with tears）— *Actually it's the same guy that wells up every time...*


### 🔽 down（向下/减少/记录）
*down 表示"向下、减少、写下"，常带有方向性下降或使固定下来的含义。*

- [ ] **go down**  — 下去；下降；发生 — *Anyway, they want me to go down to this- sonogram thing with them tomorrow.*
- [ ] **sit down**  — 坐下 — *Okay, sit down.*
- [ ] **look down**  — 往下看 — *Then I look down, and I realize there's a phone...*
- [ ] **take down**  — 取下；拆除；记下（=remove; write down）— *Well, someone was supposed to write "Rach, take down the lights" and put it on the re...*
- [ ] **get down**  — 趴下；下来 — *Get down!*
- [ ] **shut down**  — 关闭；停业（=close permanently）— *Yes! And that would be a shut-down!*
- [ ] **walk down**  — 沿着……走 — *Then we took a walk down to Bendall's, and I told him not to, but he got me a little bottle of Chanel...*
- [ ] **come down**  — 下来；下降 — *So I thought I'd just come down here, and make sure you were okay.*
- [ ] **fall down**  — 跌倒；倒塌 — *Shoot, or just fall down.*


### 📥 in（进入/内部/参与）
*in 表示"进入、在里面、参与其中"，强调由外向内的动作或处于内部状态。*

- [ ] **come in**  — 进入；进来 — *Hi, come in!*
- [ ] **live in**  — 住在（某处）— *From the nursery rhyme. 'There was a crooked man, Who had a crooked smile, Who lived in a shoe, For a... while...'*
- [ ] **get in**  — 进入；上车 — *You know, and if you get in the....*
- [ ] **go in**  — 进去 — *Boys? We're going in.*
- [ ] **work in**  — 在……工作 — *No Mom, I don't have a restaurant, I work in a restaurant.*
- [ ] **fall in**  — 掉进；陷入（fall in love） — *I mean, didn't you think you were just gonna meet somone, fall in love- and that'd be it?*
- [ ] **stay in**  — 待在家里 — *Now dad, you'll be in my room, Ronni uh, you can stay in Chandler's room.*
- [ ] **see in**  — 在……中看到（某种特质） — *Sorry, I wa- I was just saying to my friend that I thought you were the most beautiful woman that I'd ever seen in my- in my life. And then he said that- you said you thought*
- [ ] **look in**  — 探望；顺便看看（=visit briefly; drop in）— *Y'know, if you're gonna work late, I could look in on him for you.*
- [ ] **check in**  — 办理入住；报到（=register; report）— *I'm not saying he has to spend the whole evening with me, but at least check in.*

- [ ] **throw in (something)**  — 投入；额外赠送；插话（=add extra; interject）— *Um, I'm having enough trouble with the image of you and Susan together, when you throw in Tanya , yaw...*


### 🔄 out

*out 表示"向外、出去、耗尽/完成"，是 in 的反义，强调由内向外或彻底完结。*

**外出/离开 (Going out)**
- [ ] **go out**  — 出去约会；外出 — *C'mon, you're going out with the guy! There's gotta be something wrong with him!*
- [ ] **get out**  — 出去；离开 — *Anyway, I just had to get out of there, and I started wondering 'Why am I doing this, and who am I doing this for?'.*
- [ ] **come out**  — 出来；出现；结果是 — *Yeah, I think for us, kissing is pretty much like an opening act, y'know? I mean it's like the stand-up comedian you have to sit through before Pink Floyd comes out.*
- [ ] **move out**  — 搬出去 — *Well, it's a pretty big commitment, I mean, what if one of us wants to move out?*
- [ ] **walk out**  — 走出去；抛弃 — *Ever since she walked out on me, I, uh...*
- [ ] **hang out**  — 闲逛；一起玩（=spend time; chill）— *Well actually thanks, but I think I'm just gonna hang out here tonight.*
- [ ] **stay out**  — 待在外面；不回家 — *Stay out of my freezer!*
- [ ] **jump out**  — 跳出来 — *You ready to jump out the airplane without your parachute?" Come on, I can't do this!*
- [ ] **push out**  — 推出；挤出去 — *Push 'em out, push 'em out, harder, harder.*

**发现/解决 (Discovering/Solving)**
- [ ] **work out**  — 锻炼；解决；进展顺利（=exercise; resolve; go well）— *Hi, Mindy. Hi, it-it's Rachel. Yeah, I'm fine. I-I saw Barry today. Oh, yeah, yeah he-he told me. No, no, it's okay. I hope you two are very happy, I really do. Oh, oh, and Mind, y'know, if-if everything works out, and you guys end up getting married and having kids- and everything- I just hope they have his old hairline and your old nose.   Okay, I know it was a cheap shot, but I feel so much better now.*
- [ ] **find out**  — 发现；查明（=discover; learn）— *This is the way that I find out.*
- [ ] **figure out**  — 弄清楚；搞明白（=understand; work out）— *Scene: Monica's Apartment, everyone is there and watching a Spanish Soap on TV and are trying to figure out what is going on.*
- [ ] **burn out**  — 燃尽；筋疲力尽（=exhaust; fade away）— *It is. Eventually, it kind of... burns out. But hopefully, what you're left with is trust, and security, and... well, in the case of my ex-wife, lesbianism. So, you know, for all of those people who miss out on that passion... thing, there's all that other good stuff.*
- [ ] **check out (something/someone)**  — 退房；查看；打量（=look at; examine）— *Hey, did you guys check out those new hand-dryers in the bathroom?*
- [ ] **pick out (something)**  — 挑选；选出 — *Did you pick out the vowels?*

**分离/移除/其他 (separation/removal/misc)**
- [ ] **look out (for someone)**  — 小心；朝外看；照顾 — *Listen. As someone who's seen more than her fair share of bad beef, I'll tell you: that is not such a terrible thing. I mean, they're your friends, they're just looking out after you.*
- [ ] **fall out**  — 脱落；吵架 — *Well,fall out of it.*
- [ ] **make out (with someone)**  — 接吻；爱抚；辨认出（=kiss passionately）— *I wanna stay here and make out with my girlfriend!!'*
- [ ] **put out (something)**  — 熄灭（火）；伸出 — *Then put out fire by peeing, no get invited back.*
- [ ] **ask (someone) out**  — 邀请外出；约会（=invite on a date）— *You think it would be okay if I asked out your sister?*
- [ ] **rip out (something)**  — 撕掉；扯出 — *Somebody wanna help me, tryin' to rip out my heart.*

### 🔛 on

*on 表示"在……上、继续、进行中"，强调附着、持续或处于开启状态。*

**持续/进行中 (Carrying on)**
- [ ] **come on**  — 来吧；得了吧；快点 — *Oh God, come on you guys, is this really necessary?*
- [ ] **go on**  — 继续；发生 — *Ooh, I was kinda hoping that wouldn't be an issue... Scene: Monica's Apartment, everyone is there and watching a Spanish Soap on TV and are trying to figure out what is going on.*
- [ ] **hold on**  — 等一下；坚持住 — *Hold on a second, alright?*
- [ ] **live on**  — 靠……生活；继续存在 — *Yeah, it's like when you're a kid, and your parents put your dog to sleep, and they tell you it went off to live on some farm.*
- [ ] **move on**  — 继续前进；放下过去 — *He's right, man. Please. Move on. Go to China. Eat Chinese food.*
- [ ] **hang on**  — 等一下；坚持住 — *Oh, hang on, hang on.*

**涉及/从事 (Concerning/Engaging)**
- [ ] **get on (the subway)**  — 上车；继续；相处 — *Alright. Once I got on the subway, right, and it was at night, and I rode it all the way to Brooklyn... just for the hell of it.*
- [ ] **work on**  — 致力于；从事 — *No, no, I'm still working on mine.*
- [ ] **hit on (someone)**  — 搭讪；挑逗（=flirt with）— *Joey, stop hitting on her! It's her wedding day!*
- [ ] **pass on (something)**  — 传递；转交；放弃（=decline; skip）— *..You know what, I-I'd better pass on the game.*
- [ ] **focus on** ※ — 专注于※ — *Oh, yeah, that is the part to focus on, you idiot!*
- [ ] **check on (someone/something)**  — 查看；确认状态（=verify; look after）— *I want to call my apartment and check on my grandma.*
- [ ] **border on**  — 近乎；濒临（=be almost; verge on）— *'Cause I think that borders on child abuse.*
- [ ] **plan on**  — 打算；计划（=intend; count on）— *I know this is going to sound unbelievably selfish, but, were you planning on bringing up the whole baby/lesbian thing?*


### ❌ off

*off 表示"脱离、离开、关掉"，强调分离、断开或取消。*

**脱离/离开 (Taking off)**
- [ ] **take off**  — 起飞；脱下；请假 — *Um, that's really your decision, I mean, some people prefer, you know, to take off...oh whoops!*
- [ ] **get off**  — 下车；离开；摆脱 — *Get off!*
- [ ] **fall off**  — 跌落；掉落 — *If I let go of my hair, my head will fall off.*
- [ ] **blow off**  — 吹掉；放鸽子（=skip; ignore）— *We got the proofs back from that photo shoot, you know, the one with the little vegetables. Anyway, they pretty much sucked, so, I blew off the rest of the day, and I went shopping...... and I got you, I'm looking, I'm looking, I'm looking, I got you...*
- [ ] **go off**  — 离开；爆炸；响起（闹钟） — *Yeah, it's like when you're a kid, and your parents put your dog to sleep, and they tell you it went off to live on some farm.*
- [ ] **head off**  — 出发；阻止（=set out; prevent）— *Uh, okay, yeah, we could do that, but before we head off to the murder capital of the North-East, I was, uh, kinda wanting to run something by you.*
- [ ] **start off**  — 开始；出发（=begin; set out）— *Y'know, I shouldn't've, uh, asked you to start off with a monkey.*
- [ ] **live off (something/someone)**  — 靠……生活（=depend on financially）— *C'mon, you can't live off your parents your whole life.*

**断开/停止 (Disconnecting/Stopping)**
- [ ] **shut off (something)**  — 关掉（水电等） — *Uh, listen, I don't know if you tried to call me, because, uh, idiot that I am, I accidentally shut off my phone.*
- [ ] **turn off (something)**  — 关掉 — *I turned it off. Mother of God, I turned it off!*
- [ ] **swear off (something)**  — 发誓戒掉；放弃（=give up; renounce）— *I don't think that swearing off guys altogether is the answer.*


### 🔙 back（返回/后退）
*back 表示"返回、回退、回应"，强调回到原来的位置或状态。*

- [ ] **come back**  — 回来 — *If I do this for her brother, maybe Angela will come back to me.*
- [ ] **go back**  — 回去 — *"I just wanna go back to my cell."*
- [ ] **get back**  — 回来；取回 — *I should really get back to work.*
- [ ] **bring back (something/someone)**  — 带回来；使回忆起来（=return; recall）— *Yeah, well, word of advice: Bring back the comedian.*
- [ ] **call (someone) back**  — 回电话（=return a call）— *Uh- Rachel has left the building, can you call back?*
- [ ] **move back**  — 搬回去；后退 — *Yeah, well, it's a Mercedes if I move back home.*
- [ ] **send (something) back**  — 退回；送回去 — *I can't even send back soup.*
- [ ] **walk back**  — 走回去 — *And for him to have the courage to walk back in here like nothing happened...*
- [ ] **stand back**  — 往后退 — *Ok, everyone stand back.*
- [ ] **turn back**  — 往回走；使倒退 — *That'll turn back time.*

### 🔄 over（越过/覆盖/结束）
*over 表示"越过、翻转、结束"，强调从一边到另一边或从头到尾的变化。*

- [ ] **go over**  — 走过去；复习；检查 — *Are you gonna go over there?*
- [ ] **get over (something/someone)**  — 克服；从……中恢复（=recover from; overcome）— *Well, he's a big boy, he'll get over it.*
- [ ] **come over**  — 过来（到某人处） — *Right, you're not even getting your honeymoon, God.. No, no, although, Aruba, this time of year... talk about your-  -big lizards... Anyway, if you don't feel like being alone tonight, Joey and Chandler are coming over to help me put together my new furniture.*
- [ ] **stay over**  — 留宿；过夜 — *We thought since Phoebe was staying over tonight we'd have kinda like a slumber party thing. We got some trashy magazines, we got cookie dough, we got Twister...*
- [ ] **hand over (something)**  — 交出；递过去（=give; surrender）— *So, you gals wanna hand over your money now?*
- [ ] **sit over**  — 坐在那边 — *Fill these out, sit over there.*
- [ ] **run over (something/someone)**  — 碾过；快速过一遍 — *Maybe tomorrow we can rent a car and run over some puppies.*
- [ ] **put over**  — 把……放在……上 — *You know that thing you put over here with the pin in it?*
- [ ] **start over**  — 重新开始（=begin again）— *Can we start over?*
- [ ] **take over (something)**  — 接管；接手（=assume control）— *It was so wild. We told them we were the Gunnersens in room six fifteen. Only to find out the Boston Celtics had taken over the entire sixth floor!*

### 👥 together（一起/共同）

- [ ] **get together**  — 聚在一起 — *I was always rooting for you two kids to get together.*
- [ ] **live together**  — 同居 — *'S'funny, um, uh, we agreed we'd spend the rest of our lives together. Things change, roll with the punches. I believe Julia's on the table..?*
- [ ] **come together**  — 聚在一起；走到一起 — *You've just gotta figure at some point it's all gonna come together, and it's just gonna be...*
- [ ] **put together (something)**  — 组装；拼凑 — *Anyway, if you don't feel like being alone tonight, Joey and Chandler are coming over to help me put together my new furniture.*
- [ ] **work together**  — 一起工作 — *We uh, we used to work together.*
- [ ] **go together**  — 一起去；相配 — *Oh, but, could we not go together?*

### 🏃 away（离开/远离）
*away 表示"离开、远离、消失"，强调距离上的拉开或持续进行。*

- [ ] **go away**  — 走开；消失 — *Okay, now go away so we can talk about you.*
- [ ] **get away**  — 逃脱；离开 — *The most unbelievable thing has happened. Underdog has just gotten away.*
- [ ] **stay away**  — 远离 — *But to me it is not scary, 'cause I stay away from dairy....*

### 🚪 by（经过/附近/方式）
*by 表示"经过、在旁边、通过某种方式"，强调经过或途径。*

- [ ] **come by**  — 顺路拜访；获得 — *Oh, I uh, just came by to pick up my skull. Well, not mine, but...Susan: Come in.*
- [ ] **stop by**  — 顺路拜访 — *Nothing, I just thought I'd stop by..*
- [ ] **go by**  — 经过；（时间）流逝 — *Pheebs, I can't believe he hasn't kissed you yet. I mean God, by my sixth date with Paolo, I mean he had already named both my breasts! ...Ooh. Did I just share too much?*
- [ ] **live by**  — 住在附近；遵循准则 — *That's right, mister, and I don't care how old you are, as long as you're under my roof you're gonna live by my rules.*

### 🔄 around（周围/环绕）
*around 表示"周围、四处、环绕"，强调在某个区域内的移动或分布。*

- [ ] **walk around**  — 四处走动 — *Oh, it's-it's Marcel. He keeps shutting me out, y'know? He's walking around all the time dragging his hands...*
- [ ] **turn around**  — 转身；调头 — *OK, turn around.*


### ➡️ through（穿过/经历/完成）
*through 表示"穿过、从头到尾经历、用……方式"，强调穿透或完整经历过程。*

- [ ] **go through (something)**  — 经历；仔细检查 — *Just, 'cause, I don't want her to go through what I went through with Carl- oh!*
- [ ] **get through (something)**  — 度过；打通电话（=survive; connect）— *How did you get through it?*
- [ ] **come through**  — 穿过；传来；成功 — *I remember the moonlight coming through the window- and her face had the most incredible glow.*
- [ ] **sit through (something)**  — 耐着性子看完（=endure; suffer through）— *I mean it's like the stand-up comedian you have to sit through before Pink Floyd comes out.*
- [ ] **put (someone) through**  — 接通电话；使经历（=connect a call）— *Helen, could you make sure we put through the paperwork on Miss Bookbinder's raise?*
- [ ] **stay through**  — 一直待到……结束 — *Some women might even stay through dinner.*


### ➡️ along（沿着/一起）
*along 表示"沿着、一起向前"，强调伴随性的顺向移动或进展。*

- [ ] **come along**  — 一起来；进展 — *Y'know what I mean, about how I'm always going out with all these women. And I always figured, when the right one comes along, I'd be able to be a stand-up guy and go the distance, y'know? Now I'm looking at my dad, thinking...*
- [ ] **get along**  — 相处；进展（=have a good relationship）— *Um, Pheebs, so, you guys just don't get along?*

### ↔️ across（穿过/对面）
*across 表示"横穿、在对面"，强调从一侧到另一侧的空间跨越。*

- [ ] **live across**  — 住在……对面 — *Me and Chandler live across the hall.*
- [ ] **go across**  — 穿过；走到对面 — *Hey, so listen, I went across the street and talked to the doorman- I got the peeper's name! Can I use the phone?*

## 三、常用句型结构

### 1. 反问/强调句型
- **"What's with you?"** — 你怎么了？
- **"What's the matter?"** — 怎么了？
- **"What's going on?"** — 发生什么事了？
- **"What's wrong?"** — 出什么问题了？
- **"What's that supposed to mean?"** — 那是什么意思？

### 2. 建议句型
- **"Why don't you...?"** — 你为什么不...？ - *"Why don't you just get a roommate?"*
- **"How about...?"** — ...怎么样？ - *"How about I'll catch up with you in the Ice Age?"*
- **"Maybe you should..."** — 也许你应该... - *"Maybe you should steer clear of the word 'dumped'."*

### 3. 表达意愿/打算
- **"I'm gonna..."** — 我打算... - *"I'm gonna go get one of those job things."*
- **"I think I'll..."** — 我想我会... - *"I think I'll just go home and think about my ex-wife."*
- **"I'd like to..."** — 我想要... - *"I'd like to propose a toast."*

### 4. 表达可能性/推测
- **"Maybe..."** — 也许... - *"Maybe I'll just stay here with Monica."*
- **"I guess..."** — 我猜... - *"I guess we've established who's staying here with Monica."*
- **"It looks like..."** — 看起来... - *"It looks like we're not sitting together."*

### 5. 表达时间
- **"It's about time..."** — 是时候... - *"It's time to change somebody's nicotine patch."*
- **"For a while now..."** — 有一阵子了... - *"For a while now, I've been wanting to..."*
- **"The other day..."** — 前几天... - *"The other day you said..."*
