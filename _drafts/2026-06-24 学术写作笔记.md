BERT论文经典学术句型分类总结
按照**摘要/引言、相关工作、模型方法、实验设计、消融分析、结论**六大论文写作模块拆分，同时标注句式功能、原文对应逻辑、可复用模板，覆盖NLP顶会通用学术表达。

**一、摘要 & 引言（创新点、痛点、贡献、成果句式）
**1. 提出新模型/框架
1. We introduce a new language representation model called XXX, which stands for XXX.
   释义：本文提出一种名为XXX的语言表征模型，全称XXX。（论文开篇标准句型）
2. In this paper, we improve XXX by proposing XXX.
   释义：本文通过提出XXX改进现有XXX方案。
3. We propose XXX, which alleviates the previously mentioned XXX constraint.
   释义：我们提出XXX，缓解前文提及的XX局限性。

**2. 指出现有方法缺陷（对比GPT/ELMo）
1. The major limitation is that standard language models are unidirectional, and this limits the choice of architectures.
   释义：现有方法核心局限是标准语言模型仅单向建模，限制了架构设计空间。
2. Such restrictions are sub-optimal for XX tasks, and could be very harmful when applying XX approaches to XX tasks.
   释义：该约束对XX任务并非最优，将XX方案用于XX任务时会带来严重性能损耗。
3. Current techniques restrict the power of the pre-trained representations, especially for the fine-tuning approaches.
   释义：现有技术约束了预训练表征的表达能力，微调范式下该问题尤为突出。

**3. 罗列论文核心贡献（bullet经典句式）
1. We demonstrate the importance of XX for XX representations.
   释义：本文验证XX对语言表征的关键作用。
2. We show that pre-trained representations reduce the need for heavily-engineered task-specific architectures.
   释义：实验证明预训练表征可大幅降低针对任务定制复杂架构的需求。
3. XXX advances the state of the art for multiple NLP tasks.
   释义：XXX在多项NLP任务上刷新SOTA。
4. The code and pre-trained models are publicly available at XX.
   释义：代码与预训练模型开源至XX地址。

**4. 量化实验成果句式
1. It obtains new state-of-the-art results on eleven natural language processing tasks, including pushing XX score to XX (X.X% absolute improvement).
   释义：在11项NLP任务取得全新SOTA，将XX指标提升至XX，绝对提升X.X个百分点。
2. Our model achieves an absolute improvement of X.X points over prior state-of-the-art methods.
   释义：相比此前最优基线，本文模型取得X.X的绝对提升。

**二、相关工作 Related Work（文献综述对比句型）
**1. 综述领域历史研究
1. There is a long history of pre-training general language representations, and we briefly review the most widely-used approaches in this section.
   释义：通用语言表征预训练已有长期研究，本节简要梳理主流方案。
2. Learning widely applicable representations of words has been an active area of research for decades, including non-neural and neural methods.
   释义：通用词表征学习是数十年热门研究方向，包含非神经与神经网络两类方法。

**2. 分类对比两类技术路线（特征式/微调式）
1. There are two existing strategies for applying pre-trained language representations to downstream tasks: feature-based and fine-tuning.
   释义：将预训练语言表征用于下游任务分为两条主流路线：基于特征、基于微调。
2. The feature-based approach, such as XXX, uses task-specific architectures that include pre-trained representations as additional features.
   释义：以XXX为代表的特征式方法，将预训练表征作为附加特征嵌入任务专属架构。
3. The fine-tuning approach, such as XXX, introduces minimal task-specific parameters, and trains on downstream tasks by fine-tuning all pretrained parameters.
   释义：以XXX为代表的微调范式仅引入少量任务参数，微调全部预训练参数适配下游任务。

**3. 横向对比前人模型 ELMo / GPT
1. Unlike XXX which uses XXX, our method adopts XXX to realize XX.
   释义：与采用XX方案的XXX不同，本文方法使用XX实现XX能力。
2. This is also in contrast to XXX, which uses a shallow concatenation of independently trained left-to-right and right-to-left LMs.
   释义：这与XXX存在本质区别，后者仅简单拼接两个独立训练的单向语言模型。
3. Prior work only transfers sentence embeddings to downstream tasks, where our model transfers all parameters to initialize end-task model parameters.
   释义：前人工作仅迁移句子向量，而本模型迁移全部参数初始化下游任务模型。

**三、模型方法 Method（架构、预训练、微调、输入表示）
**1. 整体框架描述
1. Our framework contains two steps: pre-training and fine-tuning.
   释义：本文框架分为两个阶段：预训练、微调。
2. During pre-training, the model is trained on unlabeled data over different pre-training tasks. For finetuning, the model is initialized with pre-trained parameters, and all parameters are fine-tuned using labeled downstream data.
   释义：预训练阶段基于无标注数据完成多任务预训练；微调阶段加载预训练权重，使用下游标注数据更新全部参数。
3. A distinctive feature of XXX is its unified architecture across different tasks. There is minimal difference between pre-trained architecture and downstream architecture.
   释义：XXX核心特点是跨任务统一架构，预训练结构与下游任务结构差异极小。

**2. 模型结构定义（Transformer参数描述）
1. We denote the number of layers as L, hidden size as H, and the number of self-attention heads as A.
   释义：定义L为层数、H为隐层维度、A为自注意力头数。
2. Critically, XXX Transformer uses bidirectional self-attention, while XXX uses constrained self-attention where every token can only attend to left context.
   释义：关键区别：XXX采用双向自注意力，而XXX限制token仅能访问左侧上下文。

**3. 输入表征构造句式
1. To make the model handle various downstream tasks, our input representation can unambiguously represent single sentence or sentence pairs in one token sequence.
   释义：为适配多样下游任务，本文输入表征可在单序列中清晰编码单句或句对。
2. For a given token, its input representation is constructed by summing the corresponding token, segment, and position embeddings.
   释义：单个token的输入表征由词嵌入、分句嵌入、位置嵌入三者相加得到。
3. The first token of every sequence is a special classification token [CLS]. The final hidden state of this token is used as aggregate sequence representation for classification tasks.
   释义：序列首字符为特殊分类符[CLS]，其最终隐层向量作为分类任务的全局序列表征。

**4. 预训练任务（MLM / NSP）标准句式
**（1）Masked Language Model
1. We randomly mask some percentage of input tokens, and the objective is to predict the original vocabulary id of masked words based only on context.
   释义：随机遮蔽一定比例输入token，目标仅依靠上下文预测被遮蔽词的原始词表ID。
2. To mitigate the mismatch between pre-training and fine-tuning, we do not always replace masked words with [MASK] token.
   释义：为缓解预训练与微调阶段分布不匹配，不会全部遮蔽词替换为[MASK]符号。
3. We mask 15% of all tokens at random, with three replacement strategies with fixed probability distribution.
   释义：随机遮蔽15%的token，采用三种固定概率的替换策略。

**（2）Next Sentence Prediction
1. Many downstream tasks rely on understanding sentence pair relationships, which cannot be captured by standard language modeling alone.
   释义：大量下游任务依赖理解句间关系，仅依靠标准语言建模无法捕捉该信息。
2. We construct a binary next sentence prediction task automatically generated from monolingual corpus.
   释义：基于单语语料自动构建二分类下一句预测任务。
3. 50% of samples use real subsequent sentence as positive label, and 50% use random corpus sentence as negative label.
   释义：50%样本使用真实后续句子作为正例，50%随机选取语料句子作为负例。

**5. 微调通用句式
1. Fine-tuning is straightforward since self-attention allows the model to model single text or text pairs by adjusting input and output layers.
   释义：微调流程简洁，自注意力机制仅需调整输入输出层即可适配单文本/文本对任务。
2. For each task, we plug task-specific inputs and outputs into the model and finetune all parameters end-to-end.
   释义：针对每个任务，接入任务专属输入输出层，端到端微调全部参数。
3. Token representations are fed into output layer for token-level tasks; [CLS] representation is used for sequence classification tasks.
   释义：token表征送入输出层处理序列标注任务；[CLS]向量用于序列分类任务。

**四、实验 Experiments（数据集、评估、训练设置、结果陈述）
**1. 数据集与任务介绍
1. XXX benchmark is a collection of diverse natural language understanding tasks, covering sentence-level and token-level evaluation.
   释义：XXX基准包含多样自然语言理解任务，覆盖句子级与词级评测。
2. Given a question and a passage containing the answer, the task is to predict the answer text span in the passage.
   释义：给定问题与包含答案的段落，任务是预测段落中答案对应的文本区间。

**2. 训练超参描述
1. We use a batch size of X and fine-tune for X epochs, searching learning rate from {X, X, X} on dev set.
   释义：批大小设为X，微调X轮，在验证集上从{X,X,X}中筛选最优学习率。
2. We use Adam optimizer with weight decay, warmup over the first X steps followed by linear learning rate decay.
   释义：采用带权重衰减的Adam优化器，前X步学习率预热，随后线性衰减。

**3. 实验结果表述
1. Both XXX and XXX outperform all baseline systems by a substantial margin across all tasks.
   释义：XXX与XXX在全部任务上大幅超越所有基线模型。
2. Larger models lead to consistent accuracy improvement across all datasets, even for datasets with limited labeled samples.
   释义：更大规模模型在全部数据集上稳定提升精度，即使标注样本稀缺的数据集也不例外。
3. Our single model outperforms all published ensemble systems on this benchmark.
   释义：本文单模型在该基准上优于所有已发表集成模型。

**五、消融实验 Ablation Study（控制变量、分析组件有效性）
**1. 消融实验开篇句式
1. In this section, we perform ablation experiments over multiple facets of our model to quantify their relative importance.
   释义：本节针对模型各模块开展消融实验，量化各组件的贡献度。
2. To isolate the impact of XX component, we fix all hyperparameters and only modify the target module for comparison.
   释义：为分离XX模块的作用，固定全部超参，仅修改目标模块做对照实验。

**2. 分析组件有效性
1. Removing XX task hurts performance significantly on XX, XX and XX tasks.
   释义：移除XX预训练任务后，XX、XX、XX任务性能出现显著下降。
2. The XX model performs worse than the full model on all tasks, with severe degradation on token-level tasks such as XX.
   释义：去掉XX的模型在全部任务上性能下降，在XX这类词级任务上衰减尤为严重。
3. Adding XX module brings consistent performance gain across all test datasets.
   释义：增加XX模块后，所有测试数据集均获得稳定性能提升。

**3. 模型规模、训练步数消融
1. We explore the effect of model size by training models with varying layers, hidden dimensions and attention heads.
   释义：通过训练不同层数、隐层维度、注意力头数的模型，探究模型规模的影响。
2. Increasing model parameters leads to continuous improvement even for small downstream datasets with limited training data.
   释义：扩大模型参数量仍能持续提升性能，即便下游任务训练数据量很小。

**4. 两种范式（微调/特征抽取）对比
1. The feature-based approach extracts fixed layer activations without updating model parameters during downstream training.
   释义：基于特征的方案固定模型参数，仅提取网络层激活作为下游输入。
2. The fine-tuning approach jointly optimizes all pre-trained parameters together with task-specific output layers.
   释义：微调方案联合优化全部预训练参数与任务专属输出层。

**六、结论 Conclusion（总结、价值、通用句式）
1. Recent transfer learning improvements demonstrate that unsupervised pre-training has become an indispensable component of language understanding systems.
   释义：近年迁移学习进展证明，无监督预训练已成为语言理解系统不可或缺的模块。
2. Our core contribution generalizes prior findings to deep bidirectional architectures, enabling a single pre-trained model to solve a wide range of NLP tasks.
   释义：本文核心贡献将现有结论拓展至深度双向架构，一套预训练模型即可适配海量NLP任务。
3. Empirical results show that bidirectional context modeling and dual pre-training objectives jointly bring consistent performance gains on diverse benchmarks.
   释义：实验结果表明双向上下文建模与双预训练目标结合，可在多类基准上稳定提升效果。
