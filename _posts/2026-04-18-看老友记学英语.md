# 看老友记学英语

说明
- 里面的口语短句非常适合口语。
- 整理内容的时候这几行说明保留。
- 内容必须出自《Friends》



名词
- ...的 This is my friend's machine.

时态
- 过去时 No, I just talked to them.  I thought we had them. We already knew that. when you were a kid, ... I didn't tell anybody that I knew you. I can't believe you two had sex in her dream. because I told him I was 22.
- 将来时  I'm gonna get my stuff.  I'm gonna order a pizza. She is gonna hate me. 
- 完成时 We've *just* finished Thanksgiving dinner. Have you never done this *before*? Why have I never tasted these before? How long has Chandler been in the shower? I've never looked at you that way before. Yeah, I think I've heard of that.
	- 现在完成进行时 I've been thinking......about how I'm always seeing girls on top of girls. I realize you guys have been wondering what exactly happened between Carol and me.

- 短语动词 
	- 动词+介词  We can just look for it. Do you talk about me? I don't want to look at another guy. What does she think of your little science project?
	- 形容词+介词：I'm familiar with the concept. I'm familiar with his work, yes.
- 情态动词 you have to take a course. We used to work together. I have to go to China. you don't have to fly back.
- 补语 You can just put it on the table. We want you to be happy.
