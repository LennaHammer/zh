# 看老友记学英语



名词
- ...的 This is my friend's machine.

时态
- 过去时 No, I just talked to them.  I thought we had them. We already knew that. when you were a kid, ... 
- 将来时  I'm gonna get my stuff.  I'm gonna order a pizza. She is gonna hate me. 
- 完成时 We've *just* finished Thanksgiving dinner. Have you never done this *before*? Why have I never tasted these before? How long has Chandler been in the shower?
	- 现在完成进行时 I've been thinking......about how I'm always seeing girls on top of girls.

动词
- 短语动词 
	- 动词+介词  We can just look for it.Do you talk about me? I don't want to look at another guy.
	- 形容词+介词：I'm familiar with the concept. I'm familiar with his work, yes.
- 情态动词 you have to take a course. We used to work together.
- 补语 You can just put it on the table.

形容词
- 形容词 That's funny.  this is great. That's great.That's fine. - That was amazing! - Don't be upset.
- It's too weird.
- I feel stupid
- 数字 I'm 9 years old.
- 形容词 No, I'm fine. Don't be silly.
- They were very nice boobies. 形容词 nice
- I make the best oatmeal-raisin cookies in the world. 形容词最高级
- it's much better than I know. 比较级
- I think it's the most beautiful table I've ever seen.
- That's ridiculous.
- You're 10 times prettier than she is.

副词 - 程度 This will seem a little weird. It's really pretty.  You're so funny. He's really funny.
- 方式 I can do this well.
- 程度（动词前） She absolutely loved me.
- 程度（形容词前）I'm a little shy.
- 连接词 First of all, I'm not losing.

介词
- 介词 Were you just full of it?

句型
- 疑问句 What's it look like?长什么样子？ How often does that happen? - Almost never.  What do you need?   How long are you in the city? A couple of days. - What are you doing here? - What kind of change? - What did you mean by that? - So, what do you think?
- 否定句 You're not serious. You don't know what that means to me.  I can't do this. No, I don't. I don't know. we don't have to do this. Not at all. - No, no. - And I can't wait to meet you. I don't think so. And my sister Tina can't see her husband anymore.
- 比较句 and I think I'm doing better. I am not as bad as Ross. 我没有像罗斯那么糟
- 祈使句 Come on.
- there be 句型 there's gonna be lots of other stuff.
- it 句型 - It's time for you to swoop in. - It's 2:30 in the morning. 
- 虚拟语气  I shouldn't have come. 
	- 第三类条件句  If I'd never met him.....this would've never happened. 

日常短句
- 感觉 Thank you. Thank you so much.
- 道歉 Oh, I'm sorry.
- 疑问句 What's wrong?  How's it going?  
- 建议 Why don't we invite her? Why won't you go out with her again?
- 肯定 Absolutely. Yeah, oh, Okay, All right
- 否定 No. 
- 插入语 you know,
- Good luck.

非谓语动词
- 不定式
	- 不定式（形容词后） Are we ready to play serious poker?
	- There's only one way to resolve this. 非谓语动词，不定时 to do，名词后
- 动名词
	- I'm just excited about being an aunt. 形容词 + 介词 + doing
	- See, I don't think swearing off guys altogether is the answer. I really don't.
- 现在/过去分词
	- He's, like, actually some spy working for the CIA. 非谓语动词，现在分词，名词后

从句
- 名词从句
	- 宾语从句 I think that one was Ross'. And I think, oh, that one was Ross'.
		- 形容词+从句 I am so glad you called me.
		- 动词+疑问词从句 I don't know why you're embarrassed. I just gotta figure out what I want.
	- 表语从句 That's why I was getting married. 
	- 主语从句 I think what you need is to develop a more sophisticated screening process.相当于 You need
- 状语从句 
	- 时间 when he looks at you 状语从句
	- 原因，因为 Because I think you love her. Because I don't think it's fair to the other cookies.
	- 条件句，如果 Marcel... Okay. If you press that button, you are in very, very big trouble. If you go to a hotel, you'll be doing stuff. 
- 定语从句 you'll hear everything we say. He's just some guy I work with! There are three things that you should know about me.
	- 定语从句 Everything you need to know is in that first kiss.
- 综合（含多个从句）
	- You should be with a guy who knows what he has when he has you.
	- You gotta make sure that when Paolo walks out......the first guy Rachel sees is you. 从句
	- She's got to know that you're everything he's not.
	- And he's the kind of guy who, when you're talking, he's listening...他是那种静静听你讲话的人
	- It seems that maybe you have intimacy issues......that you use your humor to keep people at a distance.从句，这相当于两个句子，中间断开。
