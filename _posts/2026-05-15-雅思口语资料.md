### 句型

功能+话题

功能

按照功能，按照话题雅思口语万能高分句型（分Part1/2/3 直接背）

一、Part 1 日常问答万能句型
1. 开头铺垫（万能套话）
	1. Well, honestly speaking… 说实话……
	2. Actually, I’d say… 我觉得……
	3. It depends, but generally… 看情况，但一般来说……
	4. To be fair… 客观来说……
	5. I’m really into… 我很喜欢……
2. 表达喜欢/不喜欢
- 喜欢
	1. I’m quite fond of… 我很喜欢……
	2. I have a passion for… 我热衷于……
	3. It’s really my cup of tea. 这很合我胃口。
- 不喜欢
	1. I’m not really a big fan of… 我不太喜欢……
	2. It’s not really my thing. 这不太适合我。
	3. I can’t stand… 我受不了……
1. 频率句型
	1. I do it on a daily/weekly basis. 我每天/每周都做。
	2. Once in a while… 偶尔……
	3. Hardly ever… 几乎不……
2. 解释原因
	1. The main reason is that… 主要原因是……
	2. Simply because… 单纯因为……
	3. What I like most is… 我最喜欢的是……


二、Part 2 人物/地点/物品/事件 通用句型
1. 开篇引入
	1. I’d like to talk about… 我想聊聊……
	2. One of the most memorable… I have ever had is… 我最难忘的一件事是……
	3. It occurred to me that I should talk about… 我想到可以聊聊……
2. 描述人物
	1. He/She is fairly tall with… 他/她很高，有着……
	2. What impresses me most about him/her is… 他/她最让我印象深刻的是……
	3. He has a great sense of humour. 他很有幽默感。
3. 描述地点
	1. It’s located in… 它坐落于……
	2. It’s surrounded by… 周围被……环绕
	3. The atmosphere there is really relaxing. 那里氛围很放松。
4. 描述物品
	1. It’s made of… 它由……制成
	2. It means a lot to me. 对我意义重大
	3. I’ve kept it for ages. 我已经保存很多年了。
5. 描述经历
	1. It happened when I was… 这事发生在我……的时候
	2. I was totally shocked/surprised at that time. 当时我特别震惊/意外。
	3. It turned out to be an unforgettable experience. 最后成了一次难忘的经历。
6. 结尾总结
	1. All in all, it’s definitely worth mentioning. 总之，这件事很值得一说。
	2. That’s basically everything I want to share. 大概就是我想分享的全部了。


三、Part 3 抽象讨论（高分思辨句型）
1. 同意/不同意
	1. I totally agree with this view. 我完全同意这个观点。
	2. I partly agree, but there are also some downsides. 我部分同意，但也有弊端。
	3. I don’t really buy this idea. 我不太认同这个说法。
2. 利弊分析
	1. On the one hand… On the other hand… 一方面……另一方面……
	2. The biggest advantage is that… 最大好处是……
	3. However, it also brings some potential problems. 但它也带来一些潜在问题。
3. 社会趋势/变化
	1. Nowadays, more and more people tend to… 如今越来越多人倾向于……
	2. Compared with the past, things have changed a lot. 和过去比，变化很大。
	3. It’s quite common in modern society. 这在现代社会很普遍。
4. 给出建议
	1. I think the best way is to… 我觉得最好的方式是……
	2. It’s better for people to… 人们最好……
	3. Governments/Individuals should take responsibility to… 政府/个人应该承担责任去……
5. 委婉表达观点（不绝对）
	1. It seems to me that… 在我看来……
	2. As far as I’m concerned… 就我而言……
	3. From my perspective… 从我的角度来看……


四、口语加分连接词（瞬间提分）
- 递进：Moreover / Besides / In addition 此外
- 转折：Nevertheless / However / Whereas 然而
- 因果：Therefore / As a result / That’s why 因此
- 举例：For instance / Such as / A typical example is 例如

一、Part 1 必背8大高频话题 句型+例句
1. 家乡 Hometown
	1. I was born and raised in a small city in China. 我在中国一座小城市土生土长。
	2. It’s not too big but it’s really comfortable to live in. 城市不大，但住着很舒服。
	3. The best part about my hometown is the local food. 我家乡最大的亮点是本地美食。
2. 居住 Accommodation
	1. I live in an apartment with my family now. 我现在和家人住在公寓里。
	2. My favourite room is my bedroom because it’s quiet and private. 我最喜欢卧室，安静又有私人空间。
	3. It’s convenient to live here with shops and subway nearby. 住这里很方便，周边有商店和地铁。
3. 兴趣爱好 Hobbies
	1. I’m really keen on listening to music in my spare time. 我空闲时间很喜欢听音乐。
	2. It helps me relax and release pressure from study. 能帮我放松，缓解学习压力。
	3. I stick to this hobby almost every day. 我几乎每天都坚持这个爱好。
4. 阅读 Reading
	1. I prefer reading light novels rather than serious books. 比起严肃书籍，我更喜欢读轻松小说。
	2. I usually read before going to bed to kill time. 我一般睡前看书打发时间。
	3. Reading broadens my horizons a lot. 阅读大大开阔了我的眼界。
5. 运动 Sports
	1. I’m not good at sports, but I like jogging. 我不擅长运动，但喜欢慢跑。
	2. It keeps me fit and energetic all day. 能保持身材，整天有活力。
	3. I only do exercise once or twice a week. 我一周只运动一两次。
6. 天气 Weather
	1. I prefer sunny days because they always put me in a good mood. 我更喜欢晴天，总能让我心情变好。
	2. I hate rainy days as it makes everything wet and gloomy. 我讨厌雨天，又潮湿又压抑。
	3. The weather here changes a lot in four seasons. 这里四季天气变化很大。
7. 周末 Weekend
	1. I tend to stay at home and chill out on weekends. 我周末一般宅家放松。
	2. Sometimes I hang out with friends to have dinner. 有时候会和朋友出去吃饭。
	3. Weekends are the best time to recharge myself. 周末是给自己充电的最好时间。
8. 手机/网络 Phone & Internet
	1. I can’t live without my smartphone nowadays. 现在我离不开智能手机。
	2. I mainly use it for chatting and studying online. 我主要用来聊天和上网学习。
	3. I try not to spend too much time on social media. 我尽量不花太多时间刷社交媒体。

二、Part 2 四大类万能套句（人物/地点/物品/事件）
1. 描述人物（朋友/家人/老师）
	- 开头 I’m going to talk about a very important person in my life.
	- 外貌 He is of medium build and always wears a warm smile. 中等身材，总是面带微笑。
	- 性格 He is patient, easy-going and always willing to help others. 有耐心、随和，乐于助人。
	- 结尾 I really feel lucky to have him in my life.
2. 描述地点（公园/餐厅/旅行地）
	- 开头 I’d like to describe a place that I visit quite often.
	- 环境 It has beautiful scenery and fresh air all year round. 风景很美，常年空气清新。
	- 感受 The atmosphere there is peaceful and relaxing. 氛围安静又治愈。
	- 结尾 It’s definitely my favourite place to escape from busy life.
3. 描述物品（礼物/旧物/电子产品）
	- 开头 Something I want to talk about is a special gift I received.
	- 外观材质 It looks simple but elegant, and it’s made of high-quality material. 简约优雅，材质很好。
	- 意义 It has special emotional value to me. 对我有特殊情感意义。
4. 描述事件（一次旅行/难忘经历）
	- 开头 I’d like to share an unforgettable experience that happened a few years ago.
	- 过程 Everything went smoothly and we had a great time. 一切都很顺利，玩得很开心。
	- 收获 I learned a lot and grew up a little from this experience. 从这次经历学到很多、有所成长。

三、Part3 高分思辨句型（直接撑时长、显逻辑）
1. 表达观点 - As far as I’m concerned, … 就我而言…… - From my point of view, … 在我看来……
2. 利弊对比 - On the one hand, it brings many benefits. On the other hand, it also has obvious drawbacks. 一方面好处多，另一方面也有明显弊端。
3. 社会趋势 - Nowadays, an increasing number of young people choose to … 如今越来越多年轻人选择……
4. 原因分析 - The main reason behind this trend is that … 这个趋势背后的主要原因是……
5. 给出建议 - I believe individuals should make an effort to … 我认为个人应该努力去……
6. 委婉不绝对 - It’s hard to say for sure, but I think … 很难绝对下定论，但我觉得……

5. Stay connected with family & friends

Part 2 (Long turn: describe a person/place/event/object; reasons + detailed examples)
Most common core reasons
1. It brings me happiness & unforgettable memories
2. It helps me learn new knowledge & life skills
3. It changes my lifestyle or my opinion greatly
4. It offers comfort when I feel upset or lonely
5. It improves my social ability to make new friends

Part 3 (Abstract discussion: society, trends, comparison; deeper general reasons + societal examples)
Most frequent general reasons
4. Technology development changes people’s habits
5. People pursue higher quality of life
6. Modern people face heavy work pressure
7. Environmental protection becomes more important
8. Education helps people compete in the job market
9. Cost and efficiency affect public choices
